Entry Date: 2022-08-22

*Semipalmated Plover on the Sodus Point shore of Lake Ontario*

---------------------------------------------------------------
## Notes
Like the sandpipers, these plover straddle the edge of the shore and water. Look like small [[Killdeer]].
